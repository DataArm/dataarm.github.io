DataArm Advantages

Own Your Data

Data Arm is a software application that runs in your own datacenter or cloud, and is delivered as an easily-updated container-based micro-service.

Constantly Observing

Probes are configured to reach out to various services (Cloud PaaS/IaaS, Networking hardware, CMDBs, virtualization,, etc) and record service topologies in a graph database, polling/pushing methods supported, including APIs and Database drivers

Deep Exploration

The Data Arm UI allows exploring relationships and data about any component, for instance:
What is IP address 192.168.10.5 connected to?
Who owns the “Billing Service” and what software components does it contain?
How many cloud instances are being consumed by the QA team?

Powerful Change Tracking

The addition, change and removal of any observed infrastructure component is tracked, allowing users to explore how IT topologies change over time (an automated change management audit)

Easily Shareable

Graphs and custom reports can be easily embedded and referenced in CMDB tools, email reports, Wikis, Enterprise Social Networks and more.
